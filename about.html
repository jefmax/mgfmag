About Us

“We need to turn up the megaphone a little more so that everyone can hear the message”

From the Greek megas (great) and foné (voice), the megaphone isn’t just a tool to amplify sound—it’s a symbol of collective expression and cultural resonance. Whether it’s rallying voices at a protest, energizing a crowd at a rally, or commanding attention in an iconic film scene, the megaphone carries messages that demand to be heard.

Inspired by this spirit, Megáfono Magazine emerges as the first digital publication dedicated to graphic and visual design on Colombia’s Atlantic coast. It’s more than an e-zine; it’s a cultural movement, a platform to amplify the voices of artists, designers, and creatives across the Caribbean, Latin America, and internationally. By showcasing bold talent and fostering collaboration, it aims to elevate local graphic culture and connect a vibrant community of creators.

In a world where design speaks louder than words, Megáfono Magazine celebrates the artistry, identity, and innovation driving cultural change. Whether you’re a graphic designer, illustrator, or cultural pioneer, this is your space. Here, every color has meaning, every line tells a story, and every idea echoes far and wide.
